If all Christian media
was put into one room...

...how would you find media that interests you?

Similarly

When ministries get
a bulk of media...

...how will their audience find what interests them?

This is a solved problem in the physical world...

Together with others,

Language codes

BCP47

Combines many well known
ISO codes into a powerful language tag

en-US English as used,
in the United States
en-GB English as used,
in the Great Britain
uk Ukrainian,
looks a lot like the UK
es-419 Spanish as spoken
in Latin America
rmn-x-HIS15999 Balkan Romani, in the
East Bulgarian dialect of the HIS
de-CH-1901 German as used in Switzerland,
orthography of 1901

You can use it everywhere

"bcp47:tr"

Categories

Global Christian Taxonomy

Specifying categories for all Christian media so that Christians all over the world can search for media catered to their needs.

Goals

  • Translated in may languages
  • Categories unlimited sub categories
  • Available YouTube / WordPress / Drupal / Your website
  • Each category will have a background image / color and icon (inherited)

You can use it everywhere

"gct:biographies-and-memoirs"
